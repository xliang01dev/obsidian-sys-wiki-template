# raw index

For shared folder-index rules, follow `templates/index-common.md`.

This folder stores source material for the wiki.
Files here are inputs and evidence, not finished knowledge pages.

## What belongs here

- clipped articles and blog posts
- transcripts
- paper notes
- copied excerpts
- rough evidence and reference material

## Local rules

- Keep source material here even after related wiki pages are created.
- Do not replace raw notes with synthesized conclusions.
- When a source produces durable understanding, update `wiki/` instead of rewriting the source.

## Workflow

1. Add source material here.
2. Extract useful concepts.
3. Update existing canonical pages in `wiki/` when possible.
4. Create a new wiki page only when the concept is truly new.
