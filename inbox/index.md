# inbox index

For shared folder-index rules, follow `templates/index-common.md`.

This folder is for quick capture: unstructured notes, ideas, tasks, and fragments that still need processing before they become stable knowledge or raw source material.

## What belongs here

- quick notes from conversations or meetings
- task ideas and to-dos that are not yet in a system
- rough outlines or brainstorms
- references to things to read, watch, or think about later
- anything you want to capture now and sort out later

## What does *not* belong here

- stable, structured knowledge pages (move those to `wiki/`)
- long-form source documents (store those in `raw/`)
- finalized project documentation (store in the appropriate project folder)

## Local rules

- Capture first, organize later — speed over structure.
- Keep individual notes focused on a single idea when possible.
- Do not treat the inbox as long-term storage; regularly empty or reduce it.
- When an item becomes stable source material, move it to `raw/`.
- When an item becomes durable understanding, update or create pages in `wiki/`.

## Workflow

1. Capture new thoughts, tasks, and references here as soon as they arise.
2. During review, for each note:
   - Clarify or summarize if needed.
   - Decide whether it belongs in `raw/`, `wiki/`, a project folder, or the trash.
3. Move or link notes out of `inbox/` once they have a clear home.
4. Aim to keep the inbox small and current, not a dumping ground.
